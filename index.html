About

Food Bot will automatically fill nearly any restaurant survey and get you your free food rewards fast.

Just follow a few simple steps:
1. Find your restaurant (You can request for a restaurant to be added in the app)
2. Choose any rating to give the restaurant from 1 - 5 stars
3. Use your camera to quickly scan the survey code.
4. Watch as Food Bot fills out the entire survey for you.
5. After finished Food Bot will save a pdf of the final survey screen and save your validation code.
Use Food Bot history tab to see a record of all surveys you have completed with Food Bot.
Check it out on the AppStore for a video demo!
