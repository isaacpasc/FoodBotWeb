Privacy Policy

Privacy is very important for Food Bot. You are never asked to enter any personal information or create an account. The only exception is when certain surveys require an email to send a validation code. in this case your email is only used to fill out the survey form and saved locally on your device. Your email is never uploaded or saved in the cloud.
